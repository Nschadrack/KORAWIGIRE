KOFAOB in full stands for Kora Forum And Other Basic mainly formed by youth after observing that some part of communities in Karongi District particularly kibuye are vulnerable due to the extreme poverty and we decided to come together to help those communities, we all believe that everyone can work and leave the category of being poor. Which means Kora wigire linked with encouragement to everyone for working hard. Under our main purposes, we can achieve it together. Beside that our environment is the most valuable gift we were given, after observing how environment is being damaged we come up with will to do our best for helping the World for protecting our environment through sensibilization and other activities we will be doing with our communities, our main purposes:

  1. Improving the living conditions of young people and women in the Ubudehe category 1
  2. Fight extreme poverty of youth and women live in Ubudehe categorie 1
  3. Promote the education of young people living in extreme poverty
  4. Environment protection
  5. Advocate with local and national authorities

Kora Forum And Other Basic mission

is to fight against extreme poverty, protecting environment and other basic rights related to vulnerable group.
